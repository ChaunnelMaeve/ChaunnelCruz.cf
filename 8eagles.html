Brief Background of Business

As major economies recover from the pandemic, trade and logistics are expected to grow exponentially. Travel and employment opportunities abroad are also expected to return to pre-pandemic levels.

Transactions, however, will be dominated by e-commerce for the convenience of customers.

Brief Background of Business

     With the foregoing, the proponents behind 8Eagle Exports Inc. have established a company that will supply local goods to distribution centers abroad where there are large concentration of Filipino expats and migrant workers. The consolidation of local goods will be done locally and shipped to distribution centers abroad for retail to Filipinos. Credibility, Good rapport and ability to hedge are critical to maintain the unimpeded supply of goods. The centers abroad will be using e-commerce and delivery services for the convenience of their clientele.

     The business aims to serve not only Filipino migrants but other nationalities who have preference for Filipino goods and food. A strategic "Hub and Spoke" distribution model will be employed for farthest reach. The owners will market the business to main distribution hubs overseas with guarantee of comeptitve priciing and timely delivery of goods from warehouses to be rented near the supply hubs. Movement of articles will be monitored for quick inventory turn-over and managing of inventory cost.

     Customer care will be a priority and a mechanism of "Plan-Do-Check-Act" will be adopted to ensure continuous improvement of services. Adaptability to new trade practices and technology will be the key to compete in the global business arena.

     Short term goal is to penetrate the distribution market in Southeast Asia; Medium term would be Asia and the Saharan continent; and, long term, North America and Europe.
